TEST YOUR RESUME!

Understand your resume better through data! Resumator is a free tool designed to let you test versions of your resume. Perfect your resume through simple data analytics. Iterate and re-iterate versions until you find the resume that best presents your skills.

REAL-TIME DATA!

Receive data as it happens. Use our heat-maps, time data, and cursor tracking to help identify key turn-offs, areas of engagement, and overall appeal of your resume. Use this data to find out what areas need changing, and what areas draw in employers the best.

FREE!
